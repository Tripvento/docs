## What is Tripvento?

[Tripvento](https://tripvento.com) ranks hotels based on **why** you're traveling — not just stars and reviews. Our API scores every hotel across 14 travel intents like romantic, family, nightlife, wellness, and more, so you can surface the right property for every trip.

**Base URL:** `https://api.tripvento.com/api/v1`
